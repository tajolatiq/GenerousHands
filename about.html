About GenerousHands

GenerousHands is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing assistance to those in need. Our mission is to connect generous donors with individuals and communities facing challenges, providing support in various forms including donations, resources, and volunteer efforts.

Founded in 2024, GenerousHands has been making a positive impact in the lives of countless people across Malaysia. We believe in the power of kindness and the importance of giving back to society.

At GenerousHands, we are committed to transparency, accountability, and making a tangible difference in the world. Join us in our mission to create a brighter future for all.

Our Aim

Our aim is to provide a free platform for individuals to donate used items instead of discarding them. We believe that every used item deserves a second home and can be valuable to someone in need. By facilitating the donation process, we aim to reduce waste, support sustainability efforts, and make a positive impact on communities.
